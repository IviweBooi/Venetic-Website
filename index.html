Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes. We design according to your space, budget, preferred finish, and storage needs.

Do you do site measurements?

Yes. We can visit your space to take measurements before preparing a design and quote.

How much deposit is required?

A deposit is required before production starts. The amount depends on the size of the project.

Do you supply boards and materials?

Yes. We supply melamine boards, post-form countertops, hardware, cutting, and edging services.

How long does installation take?

Timing depends on the project size. Smaller jobs are usually quicker, while kitchens and larger installations may take longer.

Can I request only board cutting and edging?

Yes. You can request cutting and edging without a full installation service.
